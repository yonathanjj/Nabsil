About us

NABSIL Grain Bank is an integrated
agricultural solutions provider working to
transform and uplift the grain
ecosystem across Africa.

Vision

To be the leading agricultural value chain innovator in Africa, ensuring food security and economic empowerment for all stakeholders.

NABSIL Grain Bank is committed to placing farmers at the heart of our operations, delivering convenient, transparent, and secure services with efficiency and integrity.

Values
Mission

To eliminate post-harvest losses, provide financial access, and create a grain trading platform through advanced storage solutions and a digital trading platform.
